# Business glossary (banking)

Shared definitions used across reports. Report-specific field definitions live in each report's
`dictionary/data-dictionary.md`; this file holds cross-report business concepts.

## Channels & branches

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Channel** | The route a customer uses to transact or be served — Branch Teller, ATM, Internet Banking, Mobile App, Call Center, Relationship Manager. |
| **Channel type** | `Physical` (branch, ATM), `Digital` (internet, mobile), or `Assisted` (call center, RM). |
| **Branch** | A physical outlet. Classified by **type** (Flagship / Standard / Self-Service / Express) and **tier** (Tier 1–3 by size/importance). |
| **Footfall** | Count of customers physically served at a branch/channel (here: *Customers Served*). |
| **Teller throughput** | Transactions processed per staffed hour — a productivity measure for staffed channels. |
| **Cost to serve** | Processing cost per customer served. |
| **Digital adoption** | Share of activity (transactions) that flows through digital channels. |

## Service quality

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **NPS (Net Promoter Score)** | `% Promoters − % Detractors`, scored −100…+100. Promoters rate 9–10, Passives 7–8, Detractors 0–6. |
| **CSAT** | Customer satisfaction, typically a 1–5 rating; reported here as the average score. |
| **Complaint resolution rate** | Complaints resolved ÷ complaints logged. |
| **Wait time** | Time a customer waits before being served. |
| **Service time** | Time taken to complete a transaction once served. |

## Sales / cross-sell

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Cross-sell** | Selling an additional product to an existing customer. |
| **Lead** | A qualified sales opportunity generated at a branch/channel. |
| **Cross-sell conversion** | Products sold ÷ products offered. |
| **Products per customer** | Average number of products sold per customer served (relationship depth). |
| **Product** | A bank offering — Savings, Current Account, Credit Card, Personal Loan, Mortgage, Term Deposit, Insurance, Investment. Grouped into categories: Deposits, Lending, Cards, Wealth, Insurance. |

## Customer & money

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Customer segment** | Relationship tier — Mass Retail, Affluent, Private, SME, Corporate. |
| **Fee revenue** | Income earned from transaction/service fees. |
| **Currency** | Monetary amounts in this repo default to **VND** (whole numbers, no decimals) unless a report states otherwise. |

## Time

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **MoM** | Month over month (vs the previous month). |
| **YTD** | Year to date (Jan 1 → current period). |
| **Grain** | The level of detail of one fact row (e.g. *day × branch × channel*). |
